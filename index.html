The Origin

Pakistani women deserve the best.

Charlotte Tilbury doesn't have a store in Pakistan. Neither does Huda Beauty, MAC's local distribution is limited, and Fenty is practically impossible to find. Yet these are the brands Pakistani women see on every beauty influencer, every runway, every red carpet.

S&M Accessorize exists because of that gap. We work with a trusted network of sellers who purchase directly from brand counters during international sale seasons — and pass those savings on to you, with full authenticity guaranteed.

Every piece we sell is verified. Every seller in our network is vetted. And every customer gets the experience they deserve — honest, transparent, and genuinely affordable luxury.

This isn't just a resale page. It's a movement to make global beauty accessible to every girl in Pakistan.

FAQ

Common questions.

Are the products really authentic?

Absolutely. Every product is sourced directly from brand counters or authorised retailers. We never knowingly sell anything fake.

How do I pay?

EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or direct bank transfer. Payment details are shared after you confirm your order via WhatsApp.

Do you deliver to my city?

Yes — we ship nationwide via TCS and Leopards. If you're in a major city, expect delivery in 2–3 days. Other areas 3–5 days.

What is the free delivery threshold?

Orders above PKR 5,000 get completely free delivery anywhere in Pakistan. Below that, courier charges apply (PKR 200–350).

Can I return a product?

If there's an authenticity concern or the item doesn't match the description, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll sort it.

Can I request a specific product?

Yes! Send us your wishlist via WhatsApp. We'll try to source it on the next international sale season for you.
