04 ‚Äî Beauty Tips

Make Your Makeup Last All Day

Pakistan's heat is not your makeup's friend. Here's what actually works ‚Äî tested, real, no fluff.

01
Start with a proper moisturiser ‚Äî always
Dry skin eats foundation. Apply a lightweight moisturiser 5 minutes before any makeup and your foundation will grip properly, look smoother, and last hours longer. This one step changes everything.
Skin Prep
02
Primer isn't optional in Pakistan's heat
Seriously. A pore-minimising primer in summer creates a barrier between your skin and your foundation. Your makeup will survive wedding season, afternoon outings, everything. Don't skip it.
Summer Essential
03
Set your lipstick with a tissue and powder
Apply lipstick, place a tissue over your lips, and dust translucent powder over the tissue. The powder goes through the tissue fibres and sets the lipstick without changing the colour. Long-wearing perfection.
Lip Hack
04
Blot ‚Äî don't powder ‚Äî to refresh midday
Piling powder on top of oil makes your makeup look cakey and grey. Blotting paper removes the oil without disturbing your look underneath. Keep them in your bag at all times. Game changer.
Refresh Tip
05
Setting spray is your last ‚Äî and most important ‚Äî step
A good setting spray melts all your makeup layers together, prevents the powdery look, and adds a natural glow. Spray 30cm from your face in an X and T motion. Two pumps max. Your makeup will last through anything.
Final Step
06
Store preloved makeup correctly to extend life
Keep your preloved pieces away from direct sunlight and humidity. A cool drawer works perfectly. Wipe the top layer of powders before use. And always sanitise the surface of lipsticks with a cotton pad and 70% alcohol ‚Äî good as new.
